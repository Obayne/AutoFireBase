## Example: 10,000 sq ft Business Occupancy

### Input Parameters

- Area: 10,000 sq ft
- Stories: 1
- Occupancy: Business

### Calculated Results

- **Smoke Detectors**: 12 (30 ft spacing)
- **Manual Stations**: 3 (per NFPA 72)
- **Horns**: 8 (15 ft spacing)
- **Total Current**: 2.4 A
- **Battery**: 55 Ah (24 hr backup)

### Wiring Requirements

- SLC Circuit: 16 AWG, 450 ft
- NAC Circuit: 14 AWG, 300 ft
- Conduit: 3/4" EMT
